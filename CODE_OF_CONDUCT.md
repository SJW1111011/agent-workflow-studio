# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Agent Workflow Studio aims to be a respectful, practical, and truth-seeking open source project.

We want a community where people can:

- ask questions without being dismissed
- give direct technical feedback without becoming hostile
- disagree on design without making it personal
- collaborate across different tools, backgrounds, and experience levels

## Expected behavior

Please try to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent until there is strong reason not to
- explain technical disagreements with evidence
- accept correction gracefully
- keep discussions focused on the project and its users

## Unacceptable behavior

These behaviors are not acceptable in project spaces:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- hateful or discriminatory language
- deliberate intimidation or sustained hostility
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption of discussions, reviews, or collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other community spaces tied to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Moderation decisions should aim to protect contributor safety, project clarity, and healthy collaboration.

## Reporting

If you encounter serious conduct issues, please contact the maintainers through the repository owner account and include enough context for review.

## Final note

Strong opinions are welcome. Contempt is not.
